GPA CALCULATOR

Welcome to the GPA Calculator! This calculator will help you calculate your unweighted and weighted GPA. Calculating GPA and keeping track of it is important because it helps you know what you are doing well in school and what you can do better. If you want to get into a good college, having a good GPA is very important, which is why it is helpful to calculate it!

To get started, click the "Calculator" button on the navigation bar above. Click the add course button to add your classes one by one. Enter the letter grade and course type for each one. Once you're done, simply click the 'Calculate' Button and you will get your weighted and unweighted GPA displayed on screen! If you ever wish to restart, click 'Reset Table'. For any questions, click our interactive chat box at the bottom right of the Calculator Page or go to our 'More Information' page through the navigation bar. There are also resources for help on how to improve your GPA :) This page can also be found in the navigation bar above. Happy Calculating!
